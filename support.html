How to get our support

  1. Call our number: 864 469-0677
  2. Click this button.
  3. Accept the alert at the top of your browser.
  4. The Microsoft store will then start to open.
  5. If Quick Assist is installed please click Open, otherwise please click Install and Open afterwards.
  6. We will give you a 6 alphanumeric code to enter into Quick Assist under "Get Help", case does not matter.
  7. Afterwards the remote access will start and you will have to allow access.
  8. After access is granted Quick Assist will connect.
  9. We will then ask for control and you will have too click Allow at the top of your screen.
  10. We can then assist you on your computer.
