How to get our support

  2. 2. Click this button.
  3. 3. Click the "Open Microsoft Store" button at the top of your browser.
  4. 4. The Microsoft store will then start to open.
  5. 5. If Quick Assist is installed please click Open, otherwise please click Install and Open afterwards.

  9. 9. We will then ask for control and you will have to click Allow at the top of your screen.
